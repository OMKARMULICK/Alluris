What We Offer

  • Contract Manufacturing
    Our core service revolves around contract manufacturing, where we partner with brands to produce high-quality cosmetics tailored to their specifications. Whether you're launching a new line of skincare products, cosmetics, or personal care items, we have the expertise and resources to turn your ideas into reality. From formulation development to packaging design and everything in between, we handle every step of the manufacturing process with precision and care.
  • Formulation Custom
    Stand out in the competitive cosmetics market with unique formulations crafted exclusively for your brand. Our team of experienced chemists and formulators work closely with you to develop custom formulations that meet your specific requirements, from ingredient selection to performance characteristics. We leverage the latest innovations in cosmetic science to create products that resonate with your target audience and deliver exceptional results.
  • Private Labelling
    Our private labeling services empower you to bring your vision to life. From formulation to packaging design, we work closely with you to create custom cosmetic products that reflect your brand identity. Our experienced team ensures that every detail, from ingredients to labeling, meets your specifications and exceeds industry standards.
  • Solutions Packaging
    Presentation matters, especially in the world of cosmetics. We offer a range of packaging solutions to complement your brand identity and enhance the appeal of your products. Whether you prefer sleek and minimalist designs or eye-catching, vibrant packaging, we have options to suit every style and budget. Our packaging experts will guide you through the selection process, ensuring that your products make a lasting impression on consumers.
  • Regulatory Complaince
    Navigating the complex landscape of regulatory requirements can be daunting, but with Alluris Cosmetics, you can rest assured that your products are fully compliant with industry regulations. We stay up-to-date with the latest regulations and guidelines, ensuring that every product we manufacture meets the highest safety and quality standards. From ingredient sourcing to labeling and documentation, we handle all aspects of regulatory compliance so you can focus on growing your brand.
